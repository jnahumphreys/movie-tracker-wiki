# Brainstorming Questions

Two standing questions asked during brainstorming and after Q&A sessions when building out this wiki, to surface gaps before a decision is locked in:

1. **What are you least confident about right now?**
2. **What's the biggest thing I'm missing about the situation right now? What don't I realise?**

Answers that surface a real gap should be captured in [Open Questions](/open-questions.md) rather than left in conversation.
